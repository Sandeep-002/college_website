MoUs

As part of our commitment to fostering cutting-edge research and innovation, the Centre of Excellence on Drones and Autonomous Vehicles at NIT Warangal has signed significant Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs) with two leading technology firms:

HC Robotics Private Limited, Hyderabad

This MoU establishes a collaborative framework for research and development in the areas of intelligent drone systems and autonomous surveillance technologies. The partnership will facilitate joint R&D projects, hands-on training for students and faculty, and infrastructure support through workshops and equipment.

  • MoU between NITW and HC Robotics Private Limited, Hyderabad.pdf

PHN Technology Pvt. Ltd., Pune

Under this MoU, PHN Technology will set up a dedicated Robotics Training and Research Centre at NIT Warangal. This initiative, fully funded by PHN Technology, aims to advance robotics education and innovation, offering state-of-the-art training programs and collaborative research opportunities.

These partnerships mark a major step in enhancing interdisciplinary research, skill development, and industry-academia collaboration in the fields of drones, robotics, and autonomous systems.

Aigen Labs, Hyderabad

This MoU is entered into between Aigen Labs, Hyderabad, and NIT Warangal to establish a collaborative framework for research and development in intelligent drone systems and autonomous surveillance technologies. The partnership will facilitate joint R&D projects, hands-on training for students, and infrastructure support through workshops and equipment. Aigen Labs will provide industry expertise, while NIT Warangal will contribute academic research. This collaboration aims to foster innovation and knowledge sharing. The partnership will enhance technological advancements in the field of drones and autonomous vehicles.
